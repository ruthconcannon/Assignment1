My Leaving Cert Computer Science Project : The Hydro Hub

The Hydro Hub is a smart device that encourages and tracks water consumption, supporting healthy hydration practices using the BBC micro:bit. Let's break down the main functionalities:.

Tracking Water Consumption:

Whenever you tilt the device to the right, it adds one to the waterTrack count.

It also adds this count to drinkLog.

Button A

When you press button A, it shows "Sips Taken" on the device screen, followed by the number of sips stored in drinkLog

Button B

When you press button B, the drinkLog is reset.

Drink Log Array:

The array ‘drinkLog’ stores the number of times water has been consumed each time the micro:bit is tilted to the right. It is initially an empty array.

Serial Output:

The ‘waterTrack’ is continuously output to the serial interface where the data is then stored on graphs in your ‘My Hydro Hub tab'.

Continuous Display:

It continuously displays a negative icon on the screen unless a sip is being taken, in that case it displays a tick icon until you have finished taken your sip

Sound Media Project

For this assignment we were asked to:

Record some audio about a topic, the edit the audio Audacity together with other sounds to form a coherent audio piece.

For my assignment I recorded audio of me discussing the singer the Weeknd and his rise to fame. I adjusted the volume and added a bass and tremble effect to adjust frequency. I then imported the Weeknd's song as an MP3 to play in the background, and I adjusted the volume such that it enhanced rather than overshadowed my narration. Lastly, to give the background audio a sense of depth and unity between the vocal and musical elements, I subtly applied a reverb effect.
